The path to your Ijazah

The Qur'an has always travelled from heart to heart, teacher to student, in an unbroken chain. This is the road you will walk.

  1. ١

    Foundation

    Master the letters, the Makharij and the essential rules of Tajwid until your reading is sound.

  2. ٢

    Fluency

    Recite long passages smoothly and beautifully, with the rules flowing naturally from your tongue.

  3. ٣

    Memorization

    Commit the Qur'an to heart, juz by juz, with a revision system that makes what you memorize stay.

  4. ٤

    Final review

    Recite the entire Qur'an back to the Ustadh from memory, polished and precise, cover to cover.

  5. ۝

    Ijazah

    Receive your Ijazah — the traditional certification that you carry the Qur'an correctly, joining the chain of its bearers.
